The Quantum Model of the Atom!

You followed Niels Bohr as he discovered that electrons only inhabit orbits with defined energies — and that each transition between levels releases a specific amount of energy, associated with specific frequencies of light. Each element has its own spectral signature, determined by its own energy levels. It was the first step toward quantum mechanics.
