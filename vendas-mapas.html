The arrival

You arrive at the Chemistry Tower

Backpack on your shoulder. Heart pounding. At the top of the stairs, carved doors guard four mysterious floors while a stern figure watches every student who approaches. Professor Alceu already knows: not everyone who gets here will make it inside.

The Tower stays closed. Knowledge is the key.

Professor Alceu isn't here to stop you — he protects the Tower until a student is ready for it. His guidance is clear: before you can enter, you need to build a solid foundation in Chemistry.

The turning point

Professor Rafaela places The Book of Chemistry Foundations in your hands

Thirty visual maps. Twenty-nine interactive games. One final journey.
