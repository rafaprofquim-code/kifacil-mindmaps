The Chemistry Tower

At the heart of the Science District rises the Chemistry Tower: four floors of sealed rooms, each holding a different mystery about matter, atoms and the reactions that shape the world. There are no shortcuts between one floor and the next — each door only opens for those who have already proven they understood what it was there to teach.
